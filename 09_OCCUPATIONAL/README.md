# Occupational & Industry Data Sources

## Where to Find Occupational Variables

Occupational and industry data is currently documented in:

### **04_TOXIC_CHEMICALS/**
- **GREEN_PEST_OCC_VECTOR_SOCIAL_COMPREHENSIVE.md**
  - OSHA injuries and illnesses (2016-present)
  - BLS fatal injuries
  - Workers' compensation data

### **19_ECONOMIC_INDICATORS/**
- **CBP_QCEW_COMPREHENSIVE_DOCUMENTATION.md**
  - Employment by industry (NAICS codes)
  - Establishments by industry
  - Payroll by industry
  - 1990-present

- **ECONOMIC_FINANCE_HEALTH_FACILITIES_COMPREHENSIVE.md**
  - Industry composition
  - Manufacturing, mining, construction employment
